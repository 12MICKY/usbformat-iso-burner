# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in this project should remain respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the software.

Examples of expected behavior:

- giving actionable technical feedback
- being respectful in discussions and reviews
- assuming good intent while still being precise
- keeping criticism focused on code and behavior, not people

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- insulting or dismissive language
- publishing private information without permission
- disruptive or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions, comments, or interactions that violate these expectations.
